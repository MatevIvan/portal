About Me

With over a decade of experience, Ivan is a seasoned professional in the realm of process optimization and project management, dedicated to enhancing the performance of organizations catering to millions of individuals annually. Throughout his career, Ivan has demonstrated a remarkable aptitude for collaboration, ensuring the delivery of high-quality services and programs. He possesses a robust background in training and supervising staff, fostering adherence to organizational policies and industry standards.

Work History

Ivan's journey in the professional landscape began at McDonald's in Tacoma, WA, where he served as a Crew Member from September 2013 to June 2016. Here, he honed his skills in customer service and order processing, managing a high volume of orders with precision and efficiency, while also assuming leadership responsibilities in training and supervising culinary staff.

Transitioning to Northwest Door in Puyallup, WA, as a Production Technician from May 2016 to December 2017, Ivan furthered his expertise in optimizing operational efficiency. He then moved to Sonitrol in Tacoma, WA, where he played a pivotal role in ensuring the highest level of security offerings from January 2018 to May 2018. His contributions significantly contributed to the organization's ability to provide verified electronic security solutions while maintaining consistent service quality for a yearly revenue of $12 million.

Ivan continued to expand his skill set in the construction industry, joining I&I Contractor, Inc. in Tacoma, WA, as a Field Carpenter from November 2019 to June 2020. During this time, he participated in residential developments and renovations, ensuring full adherence to client specifications and contributing to over 20 projects. His meticulous attention to detail and commitment to quality standards were evident in his work on building elements such as doors, cabinets, and trim work, spanning facilities of up to 4000 square feet.

Building upon his experience at I&I, Ivan started his own company named Matev Construction, LLC in Tacoma, WA, and worked as a Field Carpenter from July 2020 to June 2024. Here, he worked on various aspects of construction projects, including doors, cabinets, trim work, and stair systems, while also taking on leadership roles in training and mentoring apprentices. Ivan's dedication to efficiency and problem-solving was highlighted by his development of streamlined processes for organizing materials and his collaboration with project managers to ensure adherence to work plans and safety regulations.

Volunteering

Beyond his professional pursuits, Ivan remains actively engaged in his community through volunteer work at the Slavic Christian Center in Tacoma, WA, where he serves as the Bible School Media Manager, as well as other various titles, since July 2009. His commitment to serving others underscores his passion for making a positive impact both in his professional endeavors and personal life.

Skills

Academically, Ivan holds an Associate's Degree in Engineering from Tacoma Community College, completed in June 2016. Building on this foundation, he pursued further education through Year Up, obtaining a Certificate in Application Development in August 2024. Through rigorous coursework encompassing languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and tools like Excel and Power BI, he has fortified his technical arsenal.
